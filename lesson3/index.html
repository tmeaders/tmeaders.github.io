Site Purpose

This site provides local and statewide weather conditions. Viewers can find daily realitive temperature ranges within a 24 hour period, in addition to 5-day and 10-day forcasts by city within each state. The intent is to aide commuters with daily driving routes, and vacationers with travel plans. Site options also include road conditions, road closures, and sunrise and sunset times.
